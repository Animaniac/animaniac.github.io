For anyone studying on this campus The Old Workshop is an ideal place to be!

The Peak District

The peak district is a national park close to Sheffield and easily accessible from Ecclesall Road. It offers amazing views and oppertunities for out door activities such as cycling, hikeing, rock-climbing & many more!

The peak district is home to many historic market and unique attractions. From national trust locations like Chatsworth house to local breweries like Thornbridge Brewery.
